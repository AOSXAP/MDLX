    Claim the ownership of the VC, if it doesn`t have an owner.

    Usage: .mclaim

  • invisible

    Turns your VC invisible.

    Usage: .mban @edi#0891

  • slots

    Changes the slots of your VC.

    Usage: .mslots 69

  • quote

    Put a quote on your level card! Put the value 0 to remove it.

    Usage: .mquote [Quote of choice]

    Sets up the servers Auto VC System!

    Usage: .mautovcset [VoiceChannel ID]
